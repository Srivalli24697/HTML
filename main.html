Chatbot

A chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate conversation with humans, either through text or voice. They are often used to automate tasks, provide customer service, or offer information in a conversational manner. Many modern chatbots utilize artificial intelligence (AI) and natural language processing (NLP) to understand user input and provide more human-like responses.

Twitter Sentimental analysis

This is an entity-level sentiment analysis dataset of twitter. Given a message and an entity, the task is to judge the sentiment of the message about the entity. There are three classes in this dataset: Positive, Negative and Neutral. We regard messages that are not relevant to the entity (i.e. Irrelevant) as Neutral.
